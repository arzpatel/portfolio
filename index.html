About me

I am a dedicated and highly skilled Analytical Chemist with a proven track record in chiral analysis of biological samples, synthesis of ionic liquids, and development of gas chromatography stationary phases. My expertise extends to a wide range of chromatographic techniques, including high-performance liquid chromatography (HPLC) and gas chromatography (GC), where I have contributed significantly to stationary phase development, ensuring precise and efficient separations.

My proficiency in various mass spectrometry (MS) techniques such as triple quadrupole MS, time of flight MS, electrospray, and matrix assisted laser desorption ionization empowers me to delve deeper into complex analytical challenges, providing accurate identification and quantification of compounds.

In addition to chromatography and MS, I am well-versed in an array of analytical chemistry techniques that encompass immunoprecipitation, circular dichroism, chiral separations, gas separation, protein purification, and extraction. My hands-on experience includes utilizing scanning electron microscopy (SEM) and UV-Visible spectrophotometry to further elucidate structural and spectroscopic details.

My career has been defined by a relentless pursuit of excellence in analytical chemistry, constantly seeking innovative solutions and utilizing diverse techniques to unravel intricate scientific puzzles. My ability to think critically and approach problems systematically has consistently resulted in comprehensive and insightful analytical interpretations.

I am passionate about pushing the boundaries of analytical chemistry, contributing my expertise to drive impactful research and development initiatives, and ultimately advancing scientific knowledge and technological frontiers.

With a strong foundation in analytical chemistry and a repertoire of specialized skills, I am poised to make substantial contributions to any project or team I am a part of.
